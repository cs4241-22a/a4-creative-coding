Gen 1 Pokédex for Pokemon GO

To get started, please press the cyan power button found on the pokédex.

The black screen is where you will encounter a new pokémon that appear next to the pokédex.

Click on the green buton to see the information of the mysterious pokémon.

This is simply a pokédex that you can cycle through to the next pokémon in the pokédex

using the blue previous button and yellow next button.

The purple M button will take you to the last entry of the pokédex where you will find Mew.
